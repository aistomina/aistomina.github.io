Step 7 - make a beverage

Having the wrong beverage can make or break your lounging experience, so choose it wisely. I personally prefer tea - any tea, but you can make just about anything you like. Preferably, it has to be pretty easy to drink. Say, if you were having a virgin mojito, the mint and fruit would keep getting in your way and distracting you from reading. And be careful, hot beverages are easy to spill all over your luxurious throw - coming from personal experience.
