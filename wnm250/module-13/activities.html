APPROACH

At Tiny Einstein Child Development Center, we believe that each child has a gift and a great potential. Our enriched curriculum covers all the possible ways of child development that would help to reveal talents and potential of the students. Professional educators and pedagogues have been working many years to create the best well-rounded, collaborative, bilingual developmental program that would enable every part of child’s brain. Every day is designed in a way that doesn’t overwhelm, but brings various interesting activities within the preschool setting.

Here is the list of enrichment classes offered by our Center within the curriculum for no additional cost. During the year School might change or bring new additional activities that are not included in this list.
